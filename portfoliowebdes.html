Quebec Tourism Website

Team Members: Giorgio Plescia, Arturo Martinez, Oleksii Redko



The Quebec Tourism Website was created to assist and inform people on traveling to Quebec. On the website you can discover factual information on the main cities, or even learn brief history of the Province.
The traveling section provides information about current events happening all around within a specific timeframe, links to the actual event page are provided. If not wanting to plan for a specific date, there are the top attractions info page to inform the visitor on the hot spot places in Quebec.
The Contact page is for us to receive feedback on our website, to further improve it based on the visitors ideas and expectations.
